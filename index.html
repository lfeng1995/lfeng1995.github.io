Brief Biography

I am a Full Professor at PALM Lab, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University, China. I am also affiliated with RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project, as a Visiting Scientist (part-time). I earned my PhD degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My main research interests lie in machine learning, data mining, and statistics, with a particular emphasis on addressing various security/reliability/robustness/privacy issues faced by multimodal large language models and AI agents.
